Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I’m having trouble installing Hotstone. Help!

A: Installation problems are usually due to low memory space. Make sure you are online and have enough free space.

Q: Is Hotstone free to play?

A: It is free to play the first 19 levels of the Summer Summits Adventure Pack and the first 9 levels of Blizzard Bluffs. Please visit our in-app store to play more!

Q: Do I need an internet connection to play HotStone?

A: No, but you’ll need one to buy map packs or comic pages or to share a achievement badges on Facebook.

Q: I bought an adventure pack, comic page, or comic book, but I deleted Hot Stone from my phone. How do I get them back?

A1: Apple devices:

  1. Make sure you are connected to the internet.
  2. Return to the Main Menu.
  3. Click ‘Options.’
  4. Scroll down and click ‘Restore Purchases.’
  5. In a few moments, your purchases should be restored.

A2: Android devices:

  1. Make sure you are connected to the internet.
  2. Start Hot Stone.
  3. In a few moments, your purchases should be restored automatically.

Q: How can I obtain more comic pages?

A: You may obtain comic pages by passing levels, purchasing them individually, or buying the entire comic book.
Note: You permanently own any pages you buy, so you can immediately restore them if you reinstall HotStone. However, reinstalling HotStone resets all of your progress, including comic page rewards for passing levels, so you will need to re-earn any pages you have not purchased.

Q: I purchased the entire comic book but can’t access pages 11-22. The message says: ‘Coming Spring 2017!’ Why?

A: Pages 1-10 are complete and are available in our first release. We are finishing pages 11-22 and will include them in an update this spring. If you purchase the entire comic book, you will automatically get the new pages when you install the update.

Q: I love your art! Is there any way I can purchase a hard copy of the comic?

A: Thank you! Our artists are tireless and passionate! Not yet, but very soon! Fill out our Contact Us form, subject "Paper Copy," and you will be the first to know when they are available.

Q: Can I reset my data and start over?

A: Absolutely. Just delete and reinstall the HotStone app. Please note that this will relock all comic reward pages and reset all badge progress. If you have purchased comic pages, those will be immediately available.

Q: I forgot what such-and-such tiles do. Where can I get help?

A: You can read about all of our tiles in the Tayalopedia.
From the Main Menu, tap 'Options' then 'Tayalopedia'
OR
During level play, pause your game by tapping anywhere, then “Options,” then “Tayalopedia.”

Q: How do I earn Secret Levels? For that matter, what is a Secret Level?

A: Secret Levels offer additional challenges and fun! A Secret Level marker will appear on a side path with a number above it. You must beat all the levels up to that path and collect that number of eyes before you can play it.

Q: Wait... what are eyes, and how do I collect them?

A: Eyes are our skills reward system. The Tayal people weave diamonds into their designs that symbolize harmony with ancestors and Gaga (tribal values).
It is possible to earn three eyes on every Hot Stone level. You earn one when you:

  1. Beat a level the first time.
  2. Beat it using less tiles than the low tile goal.
  3. Beat it under the time limit.

Q: I love Hot Stone! When will you release more Adventure Packs?

A: We have the Blizzard Bluffs, Torrential Typhoon,and Burning Peaks Adventure Packs on the horizon. These will introduce awesome new gameplay elements and tiles.

Q: I wish I could make levels of my own. Can we do that, pretty please?

A: It is very possible that a level editor will be coming in the near future...
